Course Description

This course focuses on techniques of solving certain ordinary differential equations, which occur frequently in physics and engineering. We will cover most of Chapters 1, 2, 3, 4 of the textbook, and possibly some extra material. That is, we will learn about first order differential equations and systems of such equations, second order linear equations, and stability.

Prerequisites
