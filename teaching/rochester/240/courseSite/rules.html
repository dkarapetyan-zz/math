Homework Rules for Math 240
Spring 2013


1. Homework assignments are due Wednesday, and must be submitted in class. Late homework will not receive credit without a valid excuse emailed to me well in advance of the due date.

2. Late homeworks will not be accepted for grades, except in extreme cases such as religious conflicts or family emergencies.

3. Your name must be written on all sheets handed in.

4. Please staple or clip pages together.

5. You may collaborate on homework. However:

    A. You must write on your problem set the names of all people you collaborated with. Make sure your name stands out as the author of your homework, however.

    B. Each collaborator must hand in his or her own problem set. You cannot hand in a single set as the work of several people.

    C. When collaborating, every student must work on every problem. You may not divide the problems up between yourselves.

    D. Collaboration means discussing the problems together. However, the actual writing must be done individually; everyone should submit their answers in their own words. Copying is NOT allowed, and it will be considered academic dishonesty.

6. Your lowest homework grade will be dropped.
